# Specification

## Summary
**Goal:** Display a direct contact email address on the EnrollmentSection inquiry/waitlist form so prospective athletes can reach the clinic directly.

**Planned changes:**
- Add the email address `athletes@velocityhealthclinic.com` as a visible "Or email us directly at" line near the submit button in the EnrollmentSection component
- Render the email as a clickable `mailto:` link styled consistently with the existing dark/amber visual theme

**User-visible outcome:** Prospective athletes viewing the enrollment/waitlist form will see a prominently displayed, clickable email link they can use to contact the clinic directly, without affecting the existing form fields or submit functionality.
